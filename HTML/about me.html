I want to live a more creative life.

That has been one of my aspirations since childhood and it has stuck with me through all the stages of 'adulting'. From the books I've read, to the people I've met, there has always been an eagerness to explore every type of diversity the world has to offer.

Since emigrating from the beautiful island of Barbados, my childhood aspiration has been the catalyst for me to:

  • Keep learning - I am a proud of alum of Howard University and Georgetown University
  • Work hard - It's instilled in you when you join a large Consulting firm
  • Be braver - Accidently jumping out of a plane in Montreal will make you do that
  • Build resilience - When you lose your job and you have to figure shit out
  • Lose Fear - Combine the first four and you become a person who can do anything you set your mind to
