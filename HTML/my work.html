In wanting to live a more creative life, I have been very intentional in pursuing the things that spark my curiousity.

As I matriculated through graduate school, understanding and designing their customers' experiences was the primary challenge I observed brands were facing in their businesses.

Not wanting to lose the momentum and drive that comes with working through a Masters degree, I enrolled in the UX/UI Design Fbootcamp offered through Bloc, Inc a few short months afer completing grad school. With no prior design or coding experience, I embarked on one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences I have had to date.

The projects below are a compilation of the design challenges completed during my Bloc experience. Take a look and let me know what you think.

  • Blocbox

    Blocbox is a design concept for a web application which allows people to collect information and store it in a single place

  • Just A Sip

    Just A Sip is the design concept for a responsive shopping experience, simulating a monthly-subscription service for fine spirits and liquors

  • Nomables

    Nomables is an iOS app design concept that allows users to find food trucks near their location and select items for purchase
